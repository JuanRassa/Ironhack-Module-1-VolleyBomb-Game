VolleyBomb

It's a 2 player Volleyball Game.

Player 1 is on the left and Player 2 is on the right.

If the ball touches the ground on your side, the opponent scores a point.

First to score 5 points wins!

  • Player 1 controls: W, A, D
  • Player 2 controls: Up, Left, Right

Player X wins!!
